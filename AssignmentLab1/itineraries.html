Suggested Itineraries

3 Days in Paris

Perfect for first-time visitors

  • Day 1: Eiffel Tower, Seine Cruise, Trocadero
  • Day 2: Louvre, Notre-Dame area, Latin Quarter
  • Day 3: Montmartre, Sacre-Coeur, local cafes

4 Days in Tokyo

Culture and modern city highlights

  • Day 1: Asakusa, Tokyo Skytree, Ueno Park
  • Day 2: Meiji Shrine, Harajuku, Shibuya Crossing
  • Day 3: Tsukiji area, teamLab, Odaiba
  • Day 4: Day trip to Hakone or Nikko

5 Days in New York

Classic city experience

  • Day 3: Statue of Liberty, Wall Street, Brooklyn Bridge
  • Day 4: SoHo, Chelsea Market, High Line
  • Day 5: Day trip or neighborhood deep dive

4 Days in London

Historic landmarks and local neighborhoods

  • Day 3: Tower of London, Tower Bridge, Borough Market
  • Day 4: Notting Hill, Hyde Park, Camden Market
